What is Google Developer Group On Campus?

Google Developer Group On Campus (GDGOC) are community groups for college students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or postgraduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a GDGOC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their communities.
